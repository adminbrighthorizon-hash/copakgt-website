Company Overview

COPAK GENERAL TRADING (CGT) is a leading Congolese company recognized for complete industrial solutions across supply, installation, and maintenance. We support Electrical, Mechanical, Instrumentation, Electrowinning, Screen Panels, Drilling Safety Gaskets, Motors, Gearboxes, Mechanical Seals, Equipment Spares, Pumps, Valves, Reagents, Chemicals, Laboratory Equipment, PPE, and Conveyor Belt systems.
